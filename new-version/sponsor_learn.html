Educational & Engineering PCB Program

We believe that engineers and engineering students can re-shape the world. Some of the most creative and audacious electronic research comes from our students in universities or even high schools. Therefore PCBWay.com feels proud to sponsor teachers and students for their PCB needs and help engineers to finish their non-profit projects.

The biggest challenge of modern electronics is that the components are so miniaturized that students can no longer use universal boards and the parts can no longer be reliably hand soldered. By streamlining manufacturing processes, PCBWay.com is capable to fabricate your PCBs in as short as 12 hours, and offers PCB assembly under one roof as a consecutive service.

Our strong network of parts suppliers makes the sourcing quick, reliable and cost-effective.

How it works:

1.Submit your project with related photos,ideas, logs, components or any detail instructions.

2.Words to PCBWay and take photos with PCBWay

3.Evaluate based on your project information(such as the nature of your projects, how detailed your post is and other necessary supporting materials )

4.Place an order and redeem the Free Code at Checkout.

1. Engineering Projects Sponsorship

PCBWay.com is confident with our quality of our PCBs, and now we offer sponsorship for the projects from students and engineers.
For university/college students and teachers, all you need to do is use your high school address as shipping address. Please Post your projects a few details such as your university/college Name, the nature of your projects, and other necessary supporting materials to PCBWay for the sponsorship code. For engineers , all you need is to describe your projects as detailed as possible so that it can convince us that your projects is great enough and worth a sponsorship.
We will check the details of your request and send you the coupon in 24 hours. You can use the coupon at checkout.

2. Sponsorship of Competition PCBs

We value student’s competition. Please always check with us if your future orders of competition PCBs can be entitled to bigger discount or even free. This will be determined by the nature of the competition and its impact.
You may be asked to provide a little more details about the competition to us, thus we may assess your case. This is considered on a case by case basis. Try to Post your project as you may save a lot. It’s possible that all your competition PCBs will be free.

3. Shipping for sponsorship PCBs

Our sponsorship is for PCB related service we can offer, but not include the delivery. So you need to pay for the shipping charge of the sponsorship PCBs.
Try our Online Instant Quoting System to learn about the courier charge. We only use the most reliable and fastest service such as DHL, FedEx,UPS,EMS,E-Packet,HK post etc.

4. PCB Assembly Service

Though not free, PCBWay.com will offer big discount on the PCB Assembly Services for students and teachers. Please contact your customer service.

5. Discount for Future Orders

Constantly supporting our students in their PCB needs is our long term strategy. PCBWay.com offers 10-15% discount on future orders from teachers and students.
This covers the PCBs for classroom projects, team or club projects and all kinds of students’ competitions. As our original pricing is already the most competitive in the world, 10-15% discount helps students save even more in their future purchases.
Please contact us at sponsor@PCBWay.com for discount code. Before sending you the code, please kindly email us the proof of our earlier sponsorship projects or competitions.
